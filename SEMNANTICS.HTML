WHY USE SEMANTIC ELEMENTS IN HTML?

Using semantic elements is like organizing your toys into labeled boxes. It helps: Browsers: Understand and display the content correctly. Search Engines: Find and rank your page better. Assistive Technologies: Help people with disabilities navigate theweb.

COMMON SEMANTIC ELEMENTS

header: The top part of the page, like the title or logo. nav: A menu with links to other parts of the website. main: The main content or the heart of the page. section: A section of content, like a chapter in a book. article: A self-contained piece of content, like a blog post. footer: The bottom part of the page, often with contact info or copyright.
